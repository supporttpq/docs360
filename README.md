# Welcome to the Tourpaq Manual

### Overview

Welcome to the official **Tourpaq Documentation**, your go-to resource for understanding, using, and mastering the Tourpaq travel management system. Whether you're a tour operator, travel agent, or system administrator, this manual will guide you from basic setup to advanced features.

Tourpaq is designed to simplify and enhance your travel business operations. With powerful tools for booking management, customer handling, reporting, and integrations, Tourpaq helps keep your workflows smooth, efficient, and scalable.

***

### What you'll find in this documentation

* **Getting Started** – Learn how to access and set up your Tourpaq account.
* **Core Modules** – Explore key modules such as Booking, CRM, Product Management, and more.
* **User Roles & Permissions** – Understand how to manage user access and roles efficiently.
* **Integrations** – See how to integrate Tourpaq with third-party tools and services.
* **FAQs & Troubleshooting** – Find quick answers to common questions and issues.
* **Best Practices & Tips** – Discover expert advice to get the most value from the platform.

***

### Who should use this manual?

This documentation is tailored for:

* **New users** who want to get up and running quickly
* **Experienced staff** seeking deeper knowledge of advanced features
* **IT and support teams** needing configuration and troubleshooting guidance
* **Business owners and managers** looking for insights on how to maximize the use of Tourpaq
