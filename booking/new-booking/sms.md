# SMS

The SMS tab lists all messages sent to the customer.&#x20;

The information is displayed in chronological order and contains

* Send date and hour&#x20;
* Company that sends the SMS.
* Phone number of who was sent the SMS.&#x20;
* Message.&#x20;
* Status of the SMS.&#x20;
