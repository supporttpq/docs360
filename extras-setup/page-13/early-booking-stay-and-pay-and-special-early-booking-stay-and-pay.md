# Early booking/Stay and pay and Special Early Booking/Stay and pay

These features allow the supplier to give discounts to the agency for the sold products. They work in the same way as the **Early Booking/Stay and pay** and **Special Early Booking/Stay and pay** from the hotel. For more information on this you can check [Room Cost](../../hotel/page-7/room-cost.md) **Early booking cost-discount rules** section.
