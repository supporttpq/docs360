# Room Cost Rule Interactions and FAQ

### Overview

Hotel cost can be affected by several rule types at the same time.

The final result depends on filters, rule type, and whether combination options are enabled.

### Common interactions

The most common interaction points are:

* base Room Cost Rules
* Early Booking Cost Discount Rules
* Stay and Pay Cost Rules
* Hotel Extra Cost Rules
* Special Offer Cost Rules

### What to check first

When the final cost is not what you expect, check:

1. stay dates
2. booking dates
3. room type
4. board type
5. age filters
6. days-before-arrival conditions
7. whether rule combination is enabled

### FAQ

#### Why does my Room Cost rule not apply?

Most misses come from stay dates, booking dates, or room filters.

#### What is the difference between per room and per passenger?

**Per room** applies once per room and is split across occupants.

**Per passenger** applies to each passenger.

#### Why did the price list not update after my change?

Rule changes do not always trigger recalculation immediately.

Updates can take up to 30 minutes.

#### Why do I only see one hotel cost total in Profit?

The Profit tab shows the total first.

Open the detail view to see the breakdown.

#### What does Extra Cost Included do?

It reduces the base price value used in the rule.

#### Why is my Early Booking rule with Board not applied?

All passengers in the room must match the same board type.

#### Can Early Booking and Stay and Pay combine?

Yes, if the relevant combination option is enabled.

#### Can cost become negative after several discounts?

No. If a day reaches `0`, more discounts keep it at `0`.
