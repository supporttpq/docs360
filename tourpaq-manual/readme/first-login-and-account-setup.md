# First Login & Account Setup

### Overview

This page walks you through logging in for the first time, setting up your profile, and making sure your account is configured correctly before you start working.

***

### Preconditions

Before your first login, make sure:

* You have your **username** and a **temporary password**
* You know which **Brand(s)** you'll be working with — ask your administrator if unsure

***

### Step 1 — Log in

1. Open your browser and go to your Tourpaq office URL (provided by your administrator)
2. Enter your **user** and **temporary password**
3. Click **Log in**

***

### Step 2 — Change your password

On your first login it is recomanded to create a new password.

* Minimum **8 characters**
* Include at least one **uppercase letter**, one **number**, and one **special character**
* Do not reuse your temporary password

Click **Save** to confirm.

***

### Step 3 — Set up Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Tourpaq supports 2FA for extra account security. Your administrator may require this.

1.  Go to your **User Profile**&#x20;

    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (714).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
2.  Select **2FA Auth**

    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (715).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
3. Follow the steps to link an authenticator app (e.g. Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator)
4. Scan the QR code and enter the confirmation code to activate

For full details, see the [2 Factor Authentication](https://manual.tourpaq.com/2-factor-authentication) page.

{% hint style="info" %}
&#x20;**Don't skip 2FA setup** if your administrator has enabled it.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

***

### Step 4 — Check your Brand access

1. After logging in, look at the top of the screen for your active **Brand selector**
2. Confirm you can see the Brand(s) assigned to you
3. If a Brand is missing, contact your administrator to update your user permissions

***

### Step 5 — Explore your profile settings

In your **User Profile** you can:

* Update your **display name** and contact details
* Review your **role and permissions**
