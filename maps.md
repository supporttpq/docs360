# Maps

In the VisitSun mobile app, the **Maps** section (found under the Destination tab) allows users to check the map for their current booking resort. The map can be configured in Tourpaq Office under **Guest Appâ†’Maps**, where a guide or admin can define points of interest by clicking on the map and entering "Location Details". It is also possible to upload pictures for each defined point of interest.
